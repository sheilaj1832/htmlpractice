Pascal history

  • What events caused the language to be developed?
    • Niklaus Wirth designed Pascal in 1968–69 and published in 1970, as a small, efficient language intended to encourage good programming practices using structured programming and data structuring. It is named in honor of the French mathematician, philosopher and physicist Blaise Pascal.
